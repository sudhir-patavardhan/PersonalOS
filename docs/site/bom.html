Platform Economics

Bill of Materials

Complete cost model for running PersonalOS at scale — every infrastructure component, third-party integration, and on-chain cost, grounded in 2025 market pricing and the platform's architectural decisions. The live calculator below explores unit economics from first Soul to 500,000 — adjust any assumption and see costs, revenue, and margin update in real time.

Cost Categories
Data Provider
Plaid
Financial Transaktion ingestion via Konnections. Charged per connected item (bank account) per month. Dominant cost at every scale — typically 80–90% of total platform cost.
~$0.10–$0.50 / item / month
Infrastructure
Compute, DB, Redis, Workers
App servers (Exchange matching, Harvest scheduler, API), PostgreSQL, Redis cache and queues. Scales step-wise — amortizes well beyond 5k Souls.
$230 → $27,600 / month (tiered)
On-Chain
Arweave · Base Gas · RPC
Permanent Ledger writes via Irys (~$0.001/Soul/month, shared model), Claim settlement gas on Base L2 (~$0.005/Claim), and Alchemy/QuickNode for Base chain RPC.
~$0.005 / Claim + $49–$800 / month RPC
Platform
Monitoring · APNs · Email · Apple
Sentry + Datadog for observability, Apple Developer Program ($99/year), APNs push notifications (free), and transactional email. Mostly fixed overhead.
~$350–$1,100 / month (fixed + tiered)

ON-CHAIN (Base L2 / Arweave)
Arweave via Irys
(Ledger Storage)
Permanent storage of E2E encrypted Transaktion batches per Harvest. Shared model default: Irys batches many Souls' writes into single Arweave transactions — amortizes cost to ~$0.001/Soul/month. Soul-owned accounts: ~$0.003/Soul/month (ADR-0004).
Per byte; Irys batching
~$0.001 / Soul / mo
Variable
Base Chain Gas
(Claim Settlement)
Each Claim triggers one `settle()` call on the BudgetEscrow smart contract — atomically splits USDC between platform fee and Soul Wallet. Base L2 gas: ~50k gas per tx at ~0.001 gwei. PersonalOS backend owns retry logic (ADR-0005).
Per Claim transaction
~$0.005 / Claim
Variable
Base Chain Gas
(Listing Deposit)
Each Brand Listing creation triggers a `deposit()` call to lock USDC Budget into escrow. One-time per Listing, paid by Brand (or absorbed into listing fee). ~$0.005–0.01 per deposit transaction.
Per Listing; Brand-side
~$0.005 / Listing

One-Time Costs (not in monthly BOM)
Smart Contract Security Audit
BudgetEscrow.sol handles real USDC escrowed from Brands and settlement to Souls — a production audit is non-negotiable before mainnet deploy. Typical scope: 3–5 day engagement from a reputable firm (Trail of Bits, OpenZeppelin, Spearbit).
$20,000 – $80,000
Smart Contract Deployment Gas
One-time gas cost to deploy BudgetEscrow.sol and SoulWallet (ERC-4337) contracts on Base mainnet. Base L2 fees are very low — total deployment cost is negligible compared to audit.
$50 – $200
iOS App Review & Notarization
Apple App Store review typically takes 1–3 days. No fee beyond the $99/year Developer Program. Expedited review available for critical bug fixes (free, on request).
$0 (included in Dev Program)
SOC 2 / Privacy Compliance
As a platform handling financial data, SOC 2 Type II certification will be required for enterprise Brand contracts. Readiness assessment + audit. Not required for MVP but budgeted for Series A stage.
$30,000 – $100,000
