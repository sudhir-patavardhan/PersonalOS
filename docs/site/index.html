End-to-End Platform Architecture

Own your data.
Earn from it.

Souls connect their financial accounts — transactions encrypt on-device and write permanently to Arweave, where only the Soul holds the key. A personal AI running entirely on iOS computes propensity Insights from the encrypted data; only scores ever leave the device. The Exchange continuously matches Insights to Brand bids — ranked by bid × Reputation × recency — and when a Soul claims an Offer, USDC Yield settles atomically on Base chain in seconds via an immutable smart contract.

I

The Soul's Journey

Two phases. Phase 1 earns Phase 2. A Soul first comes to understand themselves better than any advertiser could — then, and only then, unlocks a marketplace where brands must reach up to them across a Yield floor they set.

Phase 1

Self-knowledge — no marketplace yet

01 · On device

Create your Soul

A WebAuthn passkey is generated in the Secure Enclave — it's both your identity and the source of your encryption key. Under 3 minutes, no password.

ADR-31 · ADR-08

02 · On device

A wallet appears, silently

A Coinbase Smart Wallet (ERC-4337) is provisioned in the background — no seed phrase, no crypto knowledge required. It's where Yield will land later.

ADR-32

03 · On device

Connect the first bank

Plaid Link opens in a WebView. Credentials go to Plaid, never to PersonalOS. This single Konnection is the entire first session.

ADR-02 · ADR-05 (Tier 1)

04 · On device

Harvest & SoulMind enrich

Raw transactions land in a sandboxed memory buffer. SoulMind's Semantic Curation Engine resolves merchants, assigns categories, attaches intent tags, and stamps compliance flags — then zeroes the plaintext.

ADR-01 · ADR-30

05 · On device → Ledger

Write to the Ledger

The enriched batch is encrypted (AES-256-GCM) and written to Arweave via Irys — permanent, immutable, readable only with your passkey-derived key.

ADR-11 · ADR-08

06 · On device

The 90-second Insight

Your first Insight fires within 90 seconds: "$816 a year on an Adobe subscription with no usage signals." This is the moment the product earns trust.

ADR-06 · ADR-23

07 · On device

A SoulProfile & Archetype form

Scores accumulate into a behavioural profile and an Archetype — e.g. "The Discerning Researcher." This is self-knowledge, not a target.

ADR-17 · ADR-23

08 · On device

Deepen — add Health, Google

Contextual prompts invite more sources. Cross-source correlations emerge: "your best glucose months are your highest Whole Foods months" — visible only to you.

ADR-05 · ADR-24 · ADR-16

09 · On device

Depth Score climbs

Each source and each month of history raises your SoulProfile Depth Score toward the 60% gate. Financial data is weighted highest.

ADR-18 · ADR-19

⟶ Across this line, all that ever traveled was noisy Insight scores + Arweave content hashes. No transaction, no merchant, no amount. The server cannot read you.  (ADR-07 · ADR-14)
Phase 2

The marketplace — unlocked at 60%

10 · Unlocked

"Your SoulProfile is ready"

Crossing 60% Depth opens Phase 2 — framed as something earned through contribution, never sold as an upgrade.

ADR-18

11 · You declare intent

Grant a Consent + set a Yield floor

You pick a Category (e.g. automotive.new_vehicle_purchase) and set the minimum Yield a brand must pay to reach you. No Consent = no offers, no matter your score.

ADR-20 · ADR-13

12 · The Exchange discovers

A Listing matches you

Brands holding Listings in your consented Category compete; the Exchange ranks them by bid × Reputation × recency and surfaces one.

ADR-21 · ADR-25

13 · Offer arrives

Claim or dismiss

You see the brand, the Yield, and an on-device match score ("matches you 96%") that the brand never sees. You're the most-informed party in the transaction.

ADR-25

14 · Settlement

USDC lands in your wallet

A Claim settles USDC Yield via BudgetEscrow.sol on Base — plus an optional brand Voucher on top. The contract's fee split is immutable; PersonalOS cannot redirect it.

ADR-33 · ADR-34 · ADR-35

15 · The accountability loop

Conversion is verified

Your next Harvest detects whether the purchase happened. Brands whose offers are claimed but never convert accumulate Reputation decay and must bid higher.

ADR-21

16 · Yours, verifiably

Withdraw

The USDC is non-custodial and on-chain. You can verify every Claim credited your wallet correctly, independent of PersonalOS.

ADR-32 · ADR-35

II

The Build Order

Build foundation-up. Each layer has a gate — a provable condition — that must hold before the next begins. The diagram reads bottom-to-top, the order you build in. The critical path is SoulMind's Semantic Curation Engine: it's the most complex component and the quality of every Insight, Archetype, and Offer depends on it.

▲ build order — each layer stands on the one beneath it

LAYER 0

Identity & Crypto Foundation

iOS app shell · Swift WebAuthn passkey + Secure Enclave · ADR-31 AES-256-GCM + PBKDF2 key derivation · ADR-08 Coinbase Smart Wallet provisioning · ADR-32

Gate to pass A Soul can be created and the same encryption key re-derived deterministically from the passkey — and never extracted from the Enclave.

LAYER 1

Harvest & Ledger — the data spine

Plaid connector (first source only) · ADR-02 SoulMind Semantic Curation Engine · ADR-30 Arweave / Irys Ledger write + read · ADR-11 Append-only shard model · ADR-10

Gate to pass A real bank's data is enriched, encrypted, written to Arweave, and read back to an identical record — with the plaintext buffer provably zeroed.

LAYER 2

Intelligence & Phase 1 value

← MVP / Phase 1 launch
SoulMind CoreML Scoring (≤5s on A12) · ADR-23 Cross-source correlation engine · ADR-24 Insight feed + 90-second first Insight · ADR-06 Differential-privacy noise on scores · ADR-14

Gate to pass The "$816 moment" reliably fires from financial data alone. This is the smallest thing worth shipping — the product people fall in love with before any marketplace exists.

LAYER 3

Breadth & Depth

Apple Health FHIR · ADR-16 Google Data Portability API · ADR-04 Setu Account Aggregator (India) · ADR-03 Tiered onboarding 2 & 3 · ADR-05 Depth Score + health exclusion gate · ADR-18/19 · ADR-15

Gate to pass Souls reliably cross 60% Depth Score with multi-source data, and health-derived insights are provably barred from any marketplace path.

LAYER 4

The Marketplace — Phase 2

← revenue
Consent model + UX · ADR-20 Server Exchange matching · ADR-25 Multi-brand bidding + Reputation · ADR-21 BudgetEscrow.sol on Base + USDC · ADR-33/35 Claim → Yield + Voucher · ADR-34

Gate to pass An end-to-end Claim settles real USDC on Base testnet, then mainnet — Consent-gated, Yield-floor-honoured, fee-split-immutable.

LAYER 5

Hardening, Scale & Sovereignty

k-anonymity k=50 floor · ADR-37 §1033 enforcement at Exchange · ADR-02 ConsentRegistry.sol + upgrade governance · ADR-35 Soul-owned Arweave opt-in · ADR-36 Passkey recovery path · ADR-31 (open)

Gate to pass External smart-contract audit and an independent privacy audit both pass before scaled launch.

★ Watch the critical path. SoulMind's Semantic Curation Engine (ADR-30) is the only component that touches raw plaintext, and it determines the quality of everything downstream. It is also proprietary IP. Resource it first and protect it — a thin SCE makes every later layer feel hollow no matter how well it's built.
III

The Test Map

Tests are grouped to mirror the build layers, so a green group is a green light to move up. One group stands apart: the trust-defining tests. If those ever fail, the product's entire premise fails with them — they are release-blocking, no exceptions.

A · Foundation
Layer 0 — identity & crypto
  • Passkey creates, asserts, and survives app reinstall via iCloud Keychain
  • AES key derivation is deterministic from the passkey; identical key every time
  • Key is never extractable from the Secure Enclave, even by PersonalOS
  • Passkey-loss recovery behaves as specified OPEN · ADR-31
B · Harvest & SCE
Layer 1 — quality of everything downstream
  • Plaid sandbox round-trip lands real transactions in the buffer
  • Merchant resolution accuracy meets target on a labeled set "WHOLEFDS #1234" → Whole Foods
  • Every source maps cleanly to the SoulTransaktion schema, lossless
  • Compliance tags are stamped: cfpb_1033 on Plaid, health_flag on Health
  • Cross-source tags correct Adobe + zero usage → zero_usage_signal
  • No plaintext Transaktion ever written to disk; buffer zeroed post-encrypt
C · Ledger
Layer 1 — Arweave permanence
  • Write → read round-trip decrypts to an identical record array
  • Append-only: a new Harvest never mutates prior shards
  • Adding a source later re-Scores history without modifying old shards
D · Intelligence
Layer 2 — SoulMind on-device
  • Scoring ≤ 5s for ~500 records on an A12 Bionic device
  • First Insight fires ≤ 90s after the first Konnection
  • DP noise bounded at ε ≤ 2.0; Soul sees unnoised, server sees noised
  • Depth Score matches spec Plaid 12mo alone = 30%
  • The "$816 moment" reproduces on seeded data
★ E · Trust-defining — release-blocking
Cross-layer — the promise itself
  • Network capture proves the server never receives raw or even ciphertext Transaktions — only hashes, scores, Claims
  • k = 50 anonymity floor: no Exchange query can return a result implying fewer than 50 Souls
  • Health hard-gate: a record with marketplace_eligible:false can never reach Listing matching
  • §1033 use-limitation is enforced at the Exchange, not just tagged
  • Smart-contract fee split is immutable; PersonalOS cannot redirect a Soul's Yield
  • Account deletion = key destruction renders the Arweave Ledger permanently unreadable
F · Marketplace
Layer 4 — Phase 2 economics
  • Consent gate: no Consent → no Offer, regardless of Insight score
  • Exchange ranking equals bid × Reputation × recency, deterministically
  • Yield floor honoured: no Offer ever surfaces below the Soul's floor
  • Claim settles correct USDC via BudgetEscrow.sol on Base testnet
  • Voucher delivers via webhook and is additive to USDC
  • Reputation decays when a Claim doesn't convert, verified via next Harvest
G · End-to-end & acceptance
Full lifecycle
  • Full onboarding completes in under 3 minutes
  • Phase 1 → 2 unlocks at exactly 60% Depth Score
  • Whole lifecycle on testnet: create → Harvest → Insight → Consent → Offer → Claim → USDC → withdraw
  • SCE degrades gracefully with only one source connected
